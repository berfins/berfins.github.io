I’m a PhD student in Applied Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Trieste, Italy, and a member of the Evolutionary Robotics and Artificial Life Lab. My research focuses on evolutionary computation, with particular interest in optimization and learning. I’ve published work on genetic programming and am especially passionate about university-level teaching.

Outside of academia, I enjoy music. My life's passion is singing, playing guitar and piano. I like learning languages and reading about evolutionary biology, psychology and neuroscience.
